THE MU DROPOUTS...

Since 1972, we have helped many thousands of Miami Students, friends & faculty perform their first skydives. Since we have started recording jumps, Miami students have performed {{ club.total_jumps }} jumps. Hundreds more have earned their skydiving license from the United States Parachute Association with the Dropouts help. We have partnered with Skydive Warren County , a fixture in the skydiving business since 1969, to ensure that our members have the best skydiving experience that we can provide.


Members of the MU Dropouts Skydiving club are held to the highest standard of safety and respect for the sport of skydiving. Everyone skydiving with us are subject to the Basic Safety Regulations set forth by the United States Parachute Association. Ensuring the safety of our members is paramount, as safe skydiving practices lead to more skydivers, and happy skydivers.
